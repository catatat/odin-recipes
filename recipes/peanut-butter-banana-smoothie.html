Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Recipe source: The Nora Cooks blog. Original description: The most amazing peanut butter banana smoothie with only 3 ingredients! Totally vegan and so easy to make. Tastes like a milkshake!

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, cut into chunks and frozen
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup vanilla soy milk, more if needed to blend
  • optional: 1-2 tablespoons natural cocoa powder
